Audience

This site is dedicated principally to 2 type of personas:

  1. For people who exercises out, principally in the parks, that is because many people arrive to this city to exercise, they live in the majority 20 or 30 min away. So, with that we going to prevent the correct use of each weather time, if it is raining they will be prepared carrying the rain clothes, also it will occur in a hot day.
  2. Another audience is going to be for tourist who are visiting Otavalo. This city is recognized around the country for its wonderful places for visit, like mountains, lakes, also canopying. Obviously these are open air activities. But there are many other places with activities inside like traditional restaurants, museums, concerts, etc. This site also is going to help to tourist the correct activity to do in this city.
